Sustainable cooking starts with an ecostove

Hello there thanks for reaching out to Eco stoves Kenya. We are manufacturers of Volcanic Rock cookstoves.

Eco stoves use reusable volcanic rocks to cook.The volcanic rocks retain heat much longer than any other fuel. Charcoal dust(lusenyente) or briquettes are used as fire starters while solar is used to power the inbuit air system that runs forced air to the stones thus increasing combustion.
